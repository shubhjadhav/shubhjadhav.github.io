About Me

Shubham Jadhav

Ever since I started realizing that data is everywhere on the internet the Inquisitiveness guided by a fresh approach to worldly matters has always been my path. Be it the questions I encounter during a travel journey, or those numerical problems in maths or physics, I always had a way of finding the solution to it. Data Science with its wide variety of data is a treasury of problems to be solved. I strongly believe that my problem-solving and decision-making skills powered by precise analytical thinking could assist in serving the field of Data Science .

Data Science Graduate Student with a love of helping others and problem solving. My strong communication skills along with my technicals skills in Data Science and Machine Learning, Data Viz, SQL, Excel and Tableau allow me to transform data into impactful insights that lead to the best business decisions.
